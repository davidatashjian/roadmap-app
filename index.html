FAQ

Is it really free?+
Yes! Every feature, every game, every study tool is completely free. We show small ads to keep the lights on. If you want to support development, there's an optional donation link.
Do I need to create an account?+
No. Your progress saves in your browser automatically. No email, no password needed.
How personalized is it?+
Very. Your answers determine timeline, resources, milestones, and study approach. A career changer gets a different plan than a college sophomore.
What paths are supported?+
Pre-Med to MD/DO, Pre-Med to CAA (Anesthesiologist Assistant), and MCAT preparation. Each path has its own personalized timeline, resources, and study tools.
How can I support this project?+
If this tool helps you, you can buy me a coffee through the Support section. Every dollar goes toward my med school tuition. But you never have to pay — everything is free.
